會員個體消費分析與RFM會員分群¶

內容概述：以CDNOW_master 1997年1月1日至1998年6月30日的會員消費資料，做2個Part的分析¶

Part 1. 會員個體消費分析：¶
(1)會員消費時間遠近分布¶
(2)會員消費頻率分布¶
(3)會員消費金額分布¶
Part 2. RFM會員分群：¶
(1)將會員依據Recency、Frequency、Monetary數值進行1~4分的分級¶
(2)依據分級找出VIP、即將流失、沉睡、忠誠4類客戶¶
(3)將R、F、M分數加總，劃分會員為7等級¶
(4)了解7個等級會員在總會員數的佔比¶

def segment_me(df):
    if df['RFM_Score']>=9:
        return'1.Can\'t Loose Them'
    elif (df['RFM_Score']>=8) and (df['RFM_Score'] <9):
        return'2.Champions'
    elif (df['RFM_Score']>=7) and (df['RFM_Score'] <8):
        return'3.Loyal'
    elif (df['RFM_Score']>=6) and (df['RFM_Score'] <7):
        return'4.Potential'
    elif (df['RFM_Score']>=5) and (df['RFM_Score'] <6):
        return'5.Promising'
    elif (df['RFM_Score']>=4) and (df['RFM_Score'] <5):
        return'6.Needs Attention'
    else:
        return'7.Require Activation'
rfm_reset['General_Segment']=rfm_reset.apply(segment_me,axis=1)
